## ðŸ“Œ Key Outcomes & Insights

- **Maruti** emerged as the **top-performing brand**, contributing the highest total and average sales among all manufacturers.
- **SUVs** dominated overall sales, contributing **~50% of total revenue**, indicating strong customer preference for larger vehicles.
- **Hatchbacks and Sedans** followed as the next most popular body types, showing balanced demand across segments.
- **C-Segment and B-Segment cars** generated the highest sales, making them the most profitable market segments.
- **Premium segment vehicles** showed comparatively lower sales volume, indicating a niche but high-value market.
- **October recorded the highest monthly sales**, suggesting festive-season demand impact.
- **April and June showed a dip in sales**, highlighting potential seasonality in the automobile market.
- Certain models consistently delivered **high average sales**, making them key revenue drivers despite limited model count.
- Brands with fewer models (like **Jeep and CitroÃ«n**) showed **lower market penetration**, reflecting limited portfolio impact.
- Interactive slicers enabled **dynamic analysis** by Make, Model, Body Type, Segment, and Month, supporting data-driven decision making.
